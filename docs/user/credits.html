Citing UCSF ChimeraX

ChimeraX is developed by the UCSF Resource for Biocomputing, Visualization, and Informatics, a Biomedical Technology Resource funded by the National Institutes of Health. NIH tracks publications that make use of our Resource and its software, and hence we appreciate your cooperation in citing the grant number as described below. Thank you!

Publications with images or results from ChimeraX should include an acknowledgment similar to the following:

Molecular graphics and analyses performed with the UCSF ChimeraX package, developed by the Resource for Biocomputing, Visualization, and Informatics at the University of California, San Francisco (supported by NIGMS P41-GM103311).

The home page http://www.rbvi.ucsf.edu/chimerax can also be cited. Publications include scientific papers, posters, films, videos, exhibits, and artwork.

For permission to use images from the ChimeraX web site, please contact chimera@cgl.ucsf.edu.
