Pseudobonds

A pseudobond is drawn to signify a connection other than a covalent bond, such as a hydrogen bond, metal coordination bond, or missing peptide segment.

A pseudobond group is a named set of pseudobonds that can be treated collectively.

The command-line specifiers hbonds and pbonds refer to hydrogen-bond pseudobonds and all pseudobonds, respectively, as well as their endpoint atoms.

Commands like color and show may require using the target p option to indicate acting on pseudobonds and not their flanking atoms. See also: style, rungs, struts
