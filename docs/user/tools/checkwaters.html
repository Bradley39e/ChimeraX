Tool: Check Waters

Check Waters lists the waters in an atomic structure and allows inspecting them individually for H-bonds and visual agreement with a corresponding density map (if any) opened separately and shown with Volume Viewer. See also: Rotamers, Altloc Explorer, Renumber Residues, Change Chain IDs, H-Bonds, view, clip

Check Waters can be started from the Volume section of the Tools menu and manipulated like other panels (more...).

The initial dialog contains a menu of the current atomic models. Designating the model of interest and clicking Apply (to keep that dialog up) or OK (to dismiss it) will open another panel with a list of the waters in the structure.

In the list, one or more waters can be chosen by clicking and dragging with the left mouse button; Ctrl-click (or command-click if using a Mac) toggles whether a water is chosen. Choosing waters in the list automatically selects them and focuses the view on their near vicinities (atoms within 4Å).

H-bonds involving waters are calculated automatically and displayed as pseudobonds unless Show hydrogen bonds is turned off (initial default on). Expanding the H-Bond Parameters section allows changing certain H-Bonds parameters; clicking Apply recalculates the H-bonds accordingly.

Clicking Delete deletes the chosen waters. Caution: deletion cannot be undone except by reopening the original structure.
